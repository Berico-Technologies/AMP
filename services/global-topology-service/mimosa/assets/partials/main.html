Overview

View the graph of interconnected brokers, clients and topics tethered by AMP concepts of Exchanges and Routes.

Topology Wizard

With little knowledge of AMQP, you can design complex event routes based on common usage patterns.

Topology Management

Manage topology constructs once they have been added by the Wizard. Specifically, administrators can manipulate:

  • Routes: map clients to exchanges for producing and consuming specific topics.
  • Exchanges: configuration required by a client to produce or consume messages.
  • Topics: a mapping of a concept (topic) or event type, to a route.
  • Clients: the user or machine producing or consuming events in AMP.
  • Brokers: a messaging broker that acts as an intermediary between clients.

Global Topology Settings

A set of tools to help administrate an AMP topology, including client and broker management, cacheing, and bulk configuration management.
